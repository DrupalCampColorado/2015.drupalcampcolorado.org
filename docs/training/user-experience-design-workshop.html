User Experience & Design Workshop

Price: 
$80.00
Room 203

This Training is 100% full at this time. 

Date

Friday, June 19th, 9:30 am - 5:30 pm

Course Description 

Do you find that you're building a site around your organization's departments instead of your end users? We’ll run you through a process for working with administrators and staff to create a website that focuses on your end users while keeping the goals of your organization in mind. You'll learn how to approach your projects with the fundamentals of user experience & design through this all day course. We’ll introduce you to a few tools and techniques we use with our clients at Aten for finding the right tone and content balance, ultimately creating a successful web experience.
 

Learning Objectives

In this hands-on workshop, we’ll walk through the first part of Aten’s strategy and design process to give you ideas for expanding your strategy and design toolkit. You’ll receive access to the templates we use at Aten. And you’ll learn how to run stakeholder workshops where each exercise builds on the previous one and helps you create a successful web experience.

Who will gain the most from this course?

Individuals that are interested in doing more upfront strategy and design work, such as freelancers, project owners and wearers of many hats. 
 

Prerequisites for this course

There are no specific prerequisites for this course, you will be able to leverage whatever existing knowledge you have and develop it further during the course of this training.
